MyBalance Privacy Policy

Effective date: [ADD DATE]

MyBalance is a personal finance summary application that helps users view account balances and receive summaries by email or text message. This policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

Information We Collect

  • Account balance and account metadata (such as account name and type) via Plaid.
  • Email address and/or phone number used for sending summaries.
  • Basic account identifiers needed to operate the app.

How We Access Financial Data

MyBalance uses Plaid to securely connect to financial institutions. Login credentials are handled by Plaid and are never stored by MyBalance. Plaid provides secure access tokens that allow retrieval of account data.

How We Use Information

  • To fetch account balances.
  • To generate balance summaries.
  • To send notifications via email or SMS (Twilio).

Data Storage and Security

Access tokens and sensitive data are stored securely and encrypted. Reasonable technical safeguards are used to protect information.

Data Sharing

MyBalance does not sell personal or financial data. Data is shared only with service providers necessary to operate the app, such as Plaid for bank connectivity and Twilio for messaging delivery.

Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as needed to provide the service. Users may request deletion of their data at any time.

Your Choices

  • You may disconnect financial accounts at any time.
  • You may opt out of SMS by replying STOP.
  • You may request data deletion by contacting support.
