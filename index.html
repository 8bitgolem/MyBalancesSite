MyBalances SMS Consent (Opt-In) Proof

This page documents how SMS consent is collected for MyBalances balance summary messages.

Opt-In Collection Method

Users provide a phone number and explicitly select SMS notifications in account preferences (notifyChannel = sms or both). Consent is not pre-checked and requires an affirmative user action.

Consent Language

By providing my phone number and choosing SMS notifications, I agree to receive SMS balance summary messages from MyBalances. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies based on my settings (daily, weekly, or monthly). Reply STOP to opt out and HELP for help. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

SMS opt-in data and consent are not shared with third parties.

Current use is for the account owner's own number in a personal testing context.
